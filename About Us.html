About Us

Be Jude is a simple hypertext game based on the POV of Jude Bellingham. You get to be in the driver’s seat of his brain and make decisions as he plays a game for his club Real Madrid. This whole idea for the website is a brainchild of my love for Real Madrid and my obligations to my coursework. My name is Jesher Olivarez, an aspiring web dev. Currently enrolled at the University of Edinburgh. We were challenged to create and develop a website as part of one of our coursework. I didn’t want to create a cliche website, that coupled with the fact that our instructor dared us to be creative and fun made me brainstorm potential website ideas.
