How To Play

Follow these instructions to immerse yourself in the thrilling world of hypertext football:

Getting Started: Select the "Kick Off" button to begin your journey as Jude Bellingham.

Choose Your Path: Upon starting the game, you'll be presented with scenarios from a match where Jude is playing for Real Madrid. You'll make decisions as Jude, influencing the outcome of the game.

Make Decisions: As Jude, you'll face various situations during the match, such as receiving the ball, defending against opponents, or having a chance to score.

Click on the options presented to decide Jude's actions.

Example decisions: Pass the ball to a teammate. Dribble towards the opposition's goal. Shoot the ball. Switch play to the wing.

Score Goals: Your ultimate goal is to guide Jude towards scoring a goal for Real Madrid. Make the right decisions and execute precise actions to achieve this.

Celebrate Like Jude: Once Jude scores a goal, you'll have the opportunity to experience his iconic celebration. Enjoy the moment of victory and immerse yourself in the excitement of the game.

Achieve Success: Keep playing and making strategic choices to improve Jude's performance and lead Real Madrid to victory. Can you unlock all of Jude's potential on the field?

Play Again: Want to relive the excitement or try different paths? Replay the game as many times as you like to explore alternate outcomes and perfect your skills as Jude Bellingham.

Enjoy your time as Jude Bellingham on the Be Jude website and immerse yourself in the world of football like never before!
